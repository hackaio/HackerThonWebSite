UDICTI, Vi LAB, Y4C  & Others Hackathon . 

People race for health; we race for Innovation. HackaIO is a collaboration project between three innovation labs at the  College of Information and Communication Technologies (CoICT)  of the University of Dar es Salaam located at Science, Kijitonyama, Dar es salaam -Tanzania. Our Goal is to build capacity for software developers, tinkers and other tech gurus, and to create awareness to non tech beings so we can collaborate together to build the better tomorrow
